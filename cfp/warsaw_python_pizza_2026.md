## Title for your talk

Knowledge representation for efficient learning and communication

## Abstract

There are many learning resources available for various fields, but they often lack a structured and precise terminology. This talk will introduce DefinIT, an open-source project that creates a terminology structured into a directed acyclic graph (DAG). DefinIT provides a hierarchy of precise, unambiguous terms for knowledge fields and academic disciplines, removing ambiguity and redundancy in concept definitions across domains. The talk will cover the principles of first-principles thinking in defining terms, the role of DAGs in structuring terminology, and practical applications for efficient learning and communication.
